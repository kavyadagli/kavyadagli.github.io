BLUE TRAIL REALTY LLC.

It is crucial that twenty-first century companies establish a strong internet presence and do so with their client base in mind.

In giving Blue Trail Realty an online identity through a website and social media platforms, I found it was essential develop a digital representation of the company that fostered a sense of trust in their potential clients.

A simple and consistent UI design which accompanies integrated MLS search allows users to get a taste for the values Blue Trail upholds while creating a platform that caters to many of a potential home-owner's needs.

MY FOOD BUDDY

Calorie counting can often be monotonous and discouraging. To remedy this sideaffect of typical calorie counting applications, a team of myself and four other developers created My Food Buddy to gamify the process of staying healthy.

My Food Buddy was built at Bitcamp, a 36 hour hackathon held at UMD. This was a perfect opportunity to improve on both our front-end and back-end development skills.

Using an agile method of development, my team was able to create a database using SQL to log user information, make an interactive Javascript module to motivate users, and put together a userfriendly interface with the hopes that My Food Buddy can bring positivity to taking care of one's body.
